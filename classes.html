What to Bring to Your CCW Class

Checklist:

  • Positive attitude, a desire to learn and a willingness to step outside of your comfort zone.
  • UNLOADED firearm* secured in a case or holster. DO NOT BRING ANY LIVE AMMUNITION INTO THE CLASSROOM!
  • 40 Cartridges of Ammunition* for your firearm. Did we mention NOT TO BRING ANY LIVE AMMUNITION INSIDE?! Factory Ammo Only. NO RELOADS.
  • Hearing Protection.*
  • Valid, State-Issued photo-Identification. (If you forget this, you WILL NOT receive a certificate until verified in person!)
  • Notepad.*
  • Pen.*
  • Highlighter.*
  • Money for remainder of balance (if any).
  • Lunch or money for lunch. (We will order lunch from a nearby eatery to maximize classroom time. Lunch prices start around $7.00. Refrigeration and microwave available.)
  • Snacks and/or drinks of preference.* (Some drinks and snacks will be provided, but it’s a long day.)
  • Weather-Appropriate Clothing. Remember, we will be shooting outdoors. Also, collarless or sleeveless shirts are not recommended unless you like smoldering hot brass landing in your belly button.
  • Eye Protection.* Not designer shades, no matter how stylish. Safety or ballistic glasses only.

*Available for purchase at class. Safety equipment and accessories: 10% OFF. Student discounts on guns and ammo vary.

What to Expect at Your CCW Class

Checklist:

Before you Arrive:

  • Be well-rested, drug and alcohol-free. If you smell like booze or pot when you show up, you will be dismissed from class without a refund. (Or we will just shoot you.)
  • Make sure your gun, gear and self are all clean and in good, safe working order.
  • Arrange for any family, friends, business or small emergencies to be handled while you are in class. Standing outside on the phone during class will not qualify you for a certificate.
  • Make sure you have transportation to and from class, and please remember that spectators are not allowed.

When you Arrive:

  • Be on time. If you are more than a few minutes late, see you at a later course date.
  • Check in at the front desk and marvel at our friendly and helpful staff.
  • Pay the remainder of your balance.
  • Leave your firearm(s) with staff to be inspected, tagged and secured.
  • Secure your other personal items as directed by staff.
  • Pick up a class packet and name tag.
  • Find a comfy place to spend the day with us.
  • Fill out and paperwork and feel free to ask for help.

During Class and On the Range:

  • Please turn off or silence cell phones.
  • Participate. Not for our sake-for yours. Please try to wait until provided opportunities for comments or questions during instructions or lectures.
  • Make sure to Understand and Obey every command from Instructors. Safety infractions are absolutely zero-tolerance. Failing to follow safety instructions is the mother of all infractions.
